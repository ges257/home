## Hi, I'm Greg.

**I build the systems that should exist but don't.**

Most engineers optimize for writing code. I optimize for validating architectural bets. My process is simple but aggressive: I deeply synthesize research, make high-conviction directional calls, and execute rapidly to prove them in production. I don't wait for the "perfect" tool—I identify the gap, architect the solution, and ship it.

---

### The Three Capabilities I Bring

1. **I Know the Science:** With an M.S. in AI and deep theoretical mastery, I understand the mathematical foundations. I push tools to their operational limits.

2. **I Act as a Truth Filter:** I filter hype. Because I understand the science, I know which tools are empty sales pitches and which ones can actually drive margin for your business.

3. **I Execute Fast:** I use "Cycle Compression" to prototype and ship custom architectures in days, not quarters.

> "You get the Safety of a scientist who knows the limits, and the Speed of a founder who builds the solution."

---

### The Intersection of Tech & Business

With years of experience as an employee, entrepreneur, and business owner—combined with a **Cornell MBA focused on Digital Transformation**—I bring deep, real-world context to the challenge of building valuable AI solutions.

I possess the strategic clarity to identify key business challenges through consultative analysis, decide exactly if and where AI creates value, and communicate technical concepts effectively to non-technical stakeholders.
